On documentation and confidence, the red and the Houston-yellow cars are stronger. The flip churn + thin records make this the highest-risk of the three.

7 · PPI / Verify Checklist

  1. Body style — coupe vs Spider, from photos / build sheet (§2). Everything else is moot until this is settled.
  2. The 2024 belt job — get Enzo's Automotive invoice; confirm variators + tensioner bearings + seals, done correctly.
  3. Emissions / CEL — it failed MO emissions 7/2024; confirm it now passes, readiness monitors set, no stored codes, cats healthy.
  4. Full PPI at a Ferrari specialist — this is the car that most needs one, given the thin paper trail. Clutch %, F1 health, leaks, suspension.
  5. Title — confirm clean IN/MT title transfers cleanly; understand the Montana-LLC link.
  6. Get the real asking price from Toyota of Muncie; aggregator prices conflict.
  7. Books, tools, both keys/remotes — often missing on multi-flip cars.
  8. Why so many hands? — ask the dealer the story; cross-check the Carketa inspection report (4/2024).
