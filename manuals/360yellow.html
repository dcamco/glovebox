3 · US-Spec — the structural advantage VIN-A

This is the single biggest point in this car's favor versus the red Euro car. Position 8 of the VIN is A ⇒ a US-delivery car.

  • No gray-market discount. US-spec is the full-value baseline — though note the measured Euro penalty on the red car is only ~$3–5k (not the folklore $15k), so this advantage is real but modest.
  • Any shop will service it. No Euro-only software/parts hesitation; standard US OBD-II, standard emissions hardware. It has passed California smog (the strictest in the US) repeatedly.
  • Broader resale pool and cleaner future exit.
  • Recalls/campaigns done: the Exhaust Variator recall (Campaign 87/97) and the OBD2-connector campaign (148) were both performed under warranty at Ferrari of San Francisco in Aug 2009 — two known 360 service-campaign items already closed out.
The catch that offsets it US-spec earns a modestly higher baseline (~$3–5k, measured) — but this car's cam belts are even older (2009 vs 2013), it has a chronic emissions/driveability history the red lacks, and the ask is $40k over fair. The spec advantage is real but small; it doesn't justify the price gap. See §5 and §8A.

9 · PPI / Verify Checklist

  1. Belt-service clock — price the engine-out belt/tensioner job; it's due immediately. Use as leverage.
  2. Clutch % via Ferrari SD/Leonardo (was ~48% in 2014); plus F1 system health, no stored faults.
  3. CEL-free scan — confirm the 2014 intake/O2/injector fix held; no rich-running codes; readiness monitors set.
  4. A/C — blows cold and holds (dye was added in 2023 to chase a leak).
  5. Parasitic draw — check the aftermarket alarm isn't still killing the battery; consider removing it.
  6. Paint-depth gauge — front & rear bumpers + left-rear quarter (documented 2022 repaint); look for bodywork behind it.
  7. Carfax — pull it; reconcile “no accidents” vs the quarter-panel repaint; confirm title chain CA→TX.
  8. Motor mounts, suspension boots, cam-cover oil seepage (noted 2009).
  9. Completeness — both keys + both remotes, owner's books, tool kit, McIntosh/Hi-Fi function.
